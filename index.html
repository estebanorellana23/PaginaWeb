Welcome

We have been in business for over 10 years and have 30+ years of experience in the Apparel Business, which has helped us to provide strong business relations and maintain all customer quality requirements and speed to market. We also own factories in Peru and the Dominican Republic.

We are located in Guatemala City, Central America. Guatemala and the US share a free trade agreement since 1989. Our country has ports in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, making transportation logistics very accessible. We also provide air services to the US through our partnerships with UPS Cargo and other major airlines.

We specialize in all types of circular knit and warp knit products. We develop and manufacture graphic tees, performance tees, polos, henleys, hoodies, and shorts/bottoms. Most of our locally produced fabrics include 100% cotton, triblends, cotton/poly blends, as well as French Terry, Fleece, and 100% Polyester.

Manufacturing

Our sewing facilities are approved by Walmart, Nike-Hurley, Disney, Universal Studios, and several other major retailers.

Global Screen Printing

We also own our screen printer facility: Global Screen Printing (8 automatic machines).

Embroidery Services

Our embroidery house has been in business for 10 years, handling customers such as Disney, Mars/M&M´S and American Eagle Outfitters to mention a few.

P&K Washing Services

Our washing facility offers garment dye (pigment/reactive), crystal, mineral washes as well as other special washes.
